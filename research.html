The Shifting Economics of Grid-Scale Storage: A 2026 Outlook

Battery storage costs have declined 40% since 2022, fundamentally altering the investment calculus for grid operators and independent power producers across OECD markets. This analysis examines the levelized cost of storage across lithium-ion, iron-air, and flow battery technologies, with projections through 2030.

Key Findings

  • 4-hour lithium-ion LCOS has fallen below $120/MWh in leading markets
  • Iron-air systems are approaching cost parity for long-duration applications
  • Merchant storage revenue stacking is viable in 6 of 12 analyzed markets
